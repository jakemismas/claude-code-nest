# Smart groups

Smart Groups are computed automatically from signals in your transcripts:

- By Pull Request: the clean, solid signal.
- By Ticket Prefix, By Git Branch, By Fork Lineage: best-effort, and often empty
  depending on your data. An empty best-effort group is expected, not an error.

These groups are strictly read-only. They never move or file a chat on their own.
When a bucket is useful, right-click it and promote it to a real folder or tag.
