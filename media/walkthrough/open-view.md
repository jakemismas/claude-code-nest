# The Nest panel

Claude Code Nest adds its own Activity Bar panel with four views for the active
workspace:

- Chats: a flat list of every session, newest first.
- Folders: a single-home hierarchy you build.
- Tags: many-to-many labels.
- Smart Groups: read-only buckets computed from your transcripts.

Everything is read-only over your Claude Code transcripts. Nest never writes,
renames, moves, or deletes anything under your sessions.
